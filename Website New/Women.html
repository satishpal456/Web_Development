Women Techmaker

Making Girls Stand up on thier Own

Google's Women Techmakers program provides visibility, community, and resources for women in technology.

Our Initiatives
From 2014 to present, Women Techmakers is continually launching global scalable initiatives and piloting new programs to support and empower women in the industry. Receive updates from us when we're rolling out new initiatives.

Learn Product Management with Alex Levich
Meet Google Product Manager Alex Levich, host of her own show on Product Management, and other notable women leaders at Google as they discuss their careers and share their insights on how and why Product Management could be your next step into the tech industry.

Public Speaking Workshop
In our first pilot for this program, 35 women from around the world were trained in improv speaking techniques. Check out their videos on YouTube and read about their experience via Newsweek. Make sure to sign-up on our form to join our next pilot initiative.

Google I/O
In 2016 Women Techmakers increased the participation of women at our annual conference for developers and technologists to 23%. Women networked the night before I/O at our annual Women Techmakers dinner, and our global, private Slack community grew. Become a member to be one of the first to hear about how to join us next year.

Global Event Series for International Women's Day
For the past three years the Women Techmakers team has convened over 200 global events across 52 countries to drive visibility, community, and resources for women in technology in honor of International Women's Day (IWD). View this year's events, and make sure to stay in touch through Membership to hear about 2017 plans.

Udacity Scholarship
Women Techmakers is excited to partner with Udacity to offer the Women Techmakers Udacity Scholarship. This initiative offered 100 full scholarships for technical certification courses to women all over the world. Our inaugural cohort started this summer and will be working together to complete one of four Google-certified Udacity nanodegrees for one year.
